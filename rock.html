Billy Joel

Billy Joel, the American music icon known for his soulful piano tunes and deep lyrics, has been a major figure in music since the '70s. Hits like "Piano Man" and "Uptown Girl" mix pop, rock, and ballads, exploring love, life, and social matters. His direct storytelling and catchy songs have made him a legend, loved by fans of all ages. Despite not dropping new pop music since 1993, Joel's impact is still felt, thanks to his live shows and lasting fame.

Queen

Formed in 1970 in London, Queen shot to fame for their unique sound and show-stopping performances. Freddie Mercury, Brian May, Roger Taylor, and John Deacon blended rock with opera, creating hits that still echo today.

Their complex harmonies and Mercury's standout voice won fans worldwide, cementing them as rock legends. Hits like "Bohemian Rhapsody" and "Don't Stop Me Now" highlight their musical daring. Despite Freddie Mercury's passing in 1991, Queen's music continues to inspire and captivate new fans.
